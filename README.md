# HR-Analytics-Dashboard
**Welcome to the HR Analytics Dashboard project! This dashboard is developed in Power BI and provides powerful visual insights into various HR metrics, focusing primarily on employee attrition, salary trends, demographics, and job satisfaction.**

**The dashboard is divided into two pages, each focusing on specific analytical aspects. Interactive slicers, navigation buttons, and performance-focused KPIs enhance user experience and help HR professionals make data-driven decisions efficiently.**

## Key Insights Derived:
- Frequent business travel increases burnout, leading to higher attrition.
- Job satisfaction and environment play a direct role in retention.
- Roles with higher salary hikes also show increased attrition, possibly due to external poaching.
- Combining Job Role + Education Field helps identify at-risk talent segments.
----------------------
##  Interactive Features:
- Back to Overview button to return to Page 1

## Key Takeaways:
- Employee attrition is strongly influenced by travel, job satisfaction, and salary changes.
- Roles like Sales Executive and HR show higher turnover despite higher hikes.
- Departments like R&D have better retention and satisfaction levels.
- Dashboard slicers and interactive filters support dynamic HR investigation.
